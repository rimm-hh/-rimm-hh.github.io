Braingomo Neurofeedback App and Web-Server

The innovative smartphone-based Braingomo-neurofeedback platform, combines our smartphone app and webserver.

The application feeds the brain data into the training games and provides direct and real-time feedback.

The web server serves as a powerful tool for clinicians to monitor the progress of their patients only by creating a profile.

Braingomo Neuro-Headset

Our self-designed headset is based on innovative dry electrodes and combined with our versatile smartphone and web applications.

Whether you are a patient suffering from epilepsy or a researcher looking to study brain activity, our headset offers you the precision and accuracy you need.

No Braingomo Neuro-Headset?

If you already have a system from Muse, Mentalab or Plux Biosignals, you can use one of those to play neurofeedback games in our app and measure your brain activities.

If not, you can order or rent one of these wireless EEG systems to be used with our app.
