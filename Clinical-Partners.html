Clinical Partners

Neurofeedback is a powerful tool that can help you achieve your goals and improve your well-being. But it's not a one-size-fits-all solution. That's why our platform works with a network of qualified and experienced clinical partners who can provide you with personalized guidance and support. They can assess your needs, design a customized protocol and monitor your progress using our innovative neurofeedback (home) training.

Don't miss this opportunity to work with the best in the field. You can shortly contact our clinical partners and see if neurofeedback is suitable for you.

Nadine Kutsche

Occupational therapist, bio- and neurofeedback therapist, hand therapist and NLP master.




Read more

For me as an occupational therapist it is especially interesting to work with the BrainGoMo, because there is a great shortage of skilled workers in our industry. As a result, we have very long waiting times. Thanks to the new possibility of teletherapy, we can let the patient train independently of us and accompany him as needed. For example, the patient can practice three times a week at home, but the therapist accompanies only one session a week. Thus, we can reach more patients and shorten waiting times. Furthermore, we sometimes have patients with very long travel distances, which makes therapy in the practice very time-consuming. These patients could start home training after an on-site familiarization phase, thus saving time and resources. Another advantage is home training in the context of neuromeditation. Since in the daily routine of the practice, ambient noise often interferes or the less familiar environment makes deep relaxation more difficult. I am looking forward to the new possibilities BrainGoMo will open up for us!

Ines Ziemann

Neurofeedback Therapist






Read more

I have been running my own bio- and neurofeedback practice since 2006, with a focus on using neurofeedback performance training to improve relaxation skills and performance. I also offer neuromeditation, mindfulness, and stress management services, as well as bio- and neurofeedback for various conditions such as chronic pain, migraines, tinnitus, mood swings, ADHD, sleep disorders, and more. To achieve long-term changes in neuronal structures, high frequency training of at least one session per week is necessary. However, this isn't always feasible in practice. Complementary home training can help avoid training interruptions and improve training success. Braingomo's mobile neurofeedback platform provides a solution to this issue. After an initial supervised training session in the practice, clients can continue training at home with the platform's support. This is particularly beneficial for people with physical limitations or those who face long journeys. It also offers flexible scheduling and reduces training interruptions.

Hany Branga

Occupational and Neurofeedback Therapist, Licensed Psychologist, Neurofeedback Trainer/Mentor at the Institute of EEG-Neurofeedback Thomas Feiner in Munich

Read more

I am a state-certified occupational therapist and licensed psychologist with a passion for neuroscience. After graduating with a bachelor's degree in psychology in Romania in 2005, I reoriented myself and started my training as an occupational therapist in 2006. Since then, I have gained extensive practical experience in my own practice and continuously pursue further education. In 2015, I became a freelance lecturer at the Institute for EEG Neurofeedback in Munich, where I work as a mentor and maintain close contact with renowned institutes worldwide. I offer personalized treatment to each patient, utilizing different concepts acquired through continuous training. Working with the brain is a challenging task as it plays a decisive role in our lives, determining our well-being on the chemical, electrical, and interpersonal levels. Neurofeedback is a technique offered by trained healthcare providers to address self-regulation and neuroplasticity, and I am committed to providing detailed education to my patients to ensure they are aware of their individualized treatment plans. The more one actively and responsibly trains their brain, the faster he/she will notice successes and the positive effects will remain stable over a longer period of time. BrainGoMo is a mobile neurofeedback that makes the connection between the experts and the users in order to speed up the training process and achieve the training goals faster.
