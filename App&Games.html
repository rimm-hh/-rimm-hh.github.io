Braingomo Neuro-feedback Application

Ensuring Long-Term Success with our Mobile Neurofeedback Platform.

The Braingomo-Neurofeedback platform provides the brain training outside of clinics for enhancing the cognitive performance and mental health ayntime, anywhere.

Braingomo Training Games

Braingomo smartphone app feeds the brain data into the training games and provides direct and real-time feedback.

Importantly, our app has many appealing game options with a reward system for increasing motivation and adherence to the ringing.

The combination of the smartphone app and wellness web server allows for the optimization of training parameters for supervised and individualized training outside of clinics;

Ensuring that users receive a training experience that is tailored to their specific needs and goals.
